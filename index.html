The beginning

Blood, gore, vibrant colors. These are the key elements that hooked me into the show Invincible. The show (released on Amazon prime) came out in a time where all media related to fiction was trying to be the next MCU, including the bone-dry humor. But then on March 26, 2021 Robert Kirkman threw us an unexpected fastball with this new animated series with an all-star cast including the famous J.K Simmons! This show was a breath of fresh air for the entertainment industry, finally a good super hero show that was animated!

Story synopsis: So far (without much spoilers) a teenager discovers him and his dad have amazing powers, flight, regeneration (to a degree), and are able to withstand even the harshness of space. Now, usually the apple doesn't fall far from the tree, but not in this story. (Anything else I say is spoilers).
